# Social Media Content Creator

Templates and patterns for social media promotion.

## Platform Formats

### Twitter/X (280 chars)
```
🚰 Did you know? [City]'s water scored [Grade] on WaterScore.

[Shocking stat about contaminants]

Check your water: waterscore.app/zip/[zip]

#WaterQuality #PFAS #DrinkingWater
```

### LinkedIn (Professional)
```
I just discovered something alarming about water quality in [City].

[3-4 sentences about the data/finding]

We built WaterScore to make this data accessible to everyone.
Free water quality reports for any US zip code.

🔗 waterscore.app

#WaterQuality #PublicHealth #DataScience
```

### Reddit (r/dataisbeautiful, r/water, r/environment)
```
Title: I analyzed water quality data for [X] cities. Here's what I found.

[OC] chart/visualization

Data source: EPA SDWIS
Tool: waterscore.app
```

### Instagram Caption
```
💧 Your water might not be as clean as you think.

[City] water quality: [Grade]
🔴 [Top concern]
🟡 [Second concern]  
🟢 [Something positive]

Free water report → link in bio

#WaterQuality #CleanWater #HealthyLiving #PFAS #WaterFilter
```
