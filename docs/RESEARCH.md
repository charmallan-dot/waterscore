# WaterScore — Market Research

## The Opportunity

- **Nobody aggregates US water quality data well.** EWG has a basic tap water lookup but it's shallow.
- **PFAS ("forever chemicals") regulation is brand new** — EPA set enforceable PFAS standards in 2024. Massive public awareness.
- **Every homebuyer, parent, and health-conscious consumer** wants to know their water quality but currently has to dig through PDFs and government websites.
- **Water filter affiliate commissions** are high — Berkey, AquaTru, whole-home systems = $30-100+ per sale.
- **Real estate agents** would pay for embeddable water quality scores in listings.

## Comparable Businesses

| Business | What They Do | Revenue | Gap We Fill |
|----------|-------------|---------|-------------|
| EWG Tap Water | Basic contaminant lookup | Nonprofit donations | Shallow data, no scoring, no lead/PFAS focus |
| BuiltWith | Tech detection for websites | $14M ARR | Same model: free data → aggregation → B2B |
| NomadList | City data for nomads | $2M+/yr | Same model: multi-source aggregation + pSEO |
| AirVisual/IQAir | Air quality by location | VC-funded | Same concept but for air — water has no equivalent |

## Revenue Model

### Year 1 Target: $1,000-5,000/mo
- Water filter affiliates (Amazon, Berkey, AquaTru)
- Google AdSense on 500K+ pages
- Premium reports ($4.99 one-time)

### Year 2 Target: $5,000-20,000/mo
- Real estate API ($49-199/mo per agent/brokerage)
- Subscription monitoring ($29/yr per household)
- Sponsored content from water filter brands

### Year 3+ Target: $20,000-100,000/mo
- Enterprise API (home inspection companies, real estate platforms)
- White-label reports
- Data licensing

## Competitive Moat
1. **Historical data** — every day of collection adds irreplaceable time-series data
2. **Normalized schema** — mapping 50,000+ water systems into one format is tedious to replicate
3. **SEO authority** — first mover on 500K+ water quality pages builds domain authority
4. **Community trust** — becoming the "go-to" for water quality is a brand moat
